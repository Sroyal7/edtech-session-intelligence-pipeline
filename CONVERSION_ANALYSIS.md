## Conversion Patterns Across All 10 Profiles

### HIGH Intent Indicators (Profiles 1, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10)
- Financial pressure (EMI, family support, household needs)
- Emotional pain (burnout, frustration, isolation)
- Concrete income target or savings goal
- Realistic timeline (3-6 months)
- Willingness to commit time/resources

### MEDIUM Intent Indicators (Profiles 3, 5, 7, 9)
- Identity motivation without financial desperation
- Family gatekeeping (need buy-in, not just individual conviction)
- Competing priorities (education, exploration, other goals)
- Knowledge gaps (AI anxiety, skill confidence)
- "Exploring" vs. "desperate" mindset

### Key Success Factors for Conversion
1. **Emotional pain + financial pain alignment** = highest urgency
2. **Family support/gating** = requires dual-persona messaging
3. **Identity + income combo** = strongest driver (self-respect + security)
4. **Timeline clarity** = shortens indecision phase
5. **Constraint + opportunity match** = removes friction

## How AI Generates These Analyses

The EdTech system processes raw consultation notes and outputs:

1. **Signal Extraction** – Identifies all 10+ data points (location, exp, income, timeline, constraints, pain points, motivations)
2. **Intent Scoring** – Weighs emotional pain + financial pressure + timeline + commitment readiness → HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW
3. **1-Line Explanation** – Synthesizes primary driver(s) and blocker(s) into a single actionable insight
4. **Primary Signal** – Highlights the single most influential factor for that specific profile
5. **Talk Track** – Generates follow-up conversation hooks based on the conversion readiness
